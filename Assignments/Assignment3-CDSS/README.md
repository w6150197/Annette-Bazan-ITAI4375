# Assignment 3: Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS)

## Objective
Explore the role of AI-powered Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS) in modern healthcare, analyze their potential future impact, and reflect on how clinicians interact with these systems to maintain clinical judgment while leveraging AI guidance.

## What I Did
- Reviewed current and emerging AI-CDSS technologies, such as Glass Health.
- Analyzed how AI can enhance diagnostic accuracy, predictive analytics, and workflow efficiency.
- Examined ethical, privacy, and bias-related considerations in AI-CDSS implementation.
- Reflected on strategies for clinicians to balance trust in AI recommendations with professional judgment.

## Weekly Discussion Insights
- CDSS assist clinicians with evidence-based decision-making through alerts, medication guidance, risk assessments, and diagnostic support.
- AI-enhanced CDSS analyze structured and unstructured data from EHRs and provide personalized, context-aware recommendations.
- Key strategies for clinician trust in AI-CDSS include:
  - **Transparency:** Explaining how AI algorithms generate recommendations.
  - **Education:** Training clinicians on AI system behavior and limitations.
  - **Clinician Control:** Ensuring override and customization options so AI supports rather than replaces human judgment.
- Question raised: As AI-powered CDSS become more accurate, how should clinicians balance trust in the system with their own clinical judgment?

## Assignment Summary
- Investigated future scenarios for AI-powered CDSS, predicting widespread integration into clinical workflows within 5–10 years.
- Explored the benefits of AI-CDSS:
  - Improved diagnostic accuracy and early disease detection.
  - Reduced clinician workload and administrative burden.
  - Enhanced guideline adherence and personalized care.
- Highlighted ethical and practical challenges:
  - Algorithmic transparency and explainability.
  - Data privacy and security.
  - Potential biases from non-representative datasets.
- Conclusion: Responsible development and governance of AI-CDSS can transform clinical decision-making while supporting clinician expertise.
