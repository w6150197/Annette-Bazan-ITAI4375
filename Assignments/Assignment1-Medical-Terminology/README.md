# Assignment 1: Medical Terminology

## Objective
Understand and critically analyze key medical terminology used in healthcare, and explore how precise terminology supports clinical decision-making and AI applications in healthcare.

## What I Did
- Completed a discussion reflecting on AI integration in healthcare, focusing on the importance of medical terminology for safe and accurate AI outputs.
- Reviewed and analyzed recent medical literature to identify 10 key medical terms, including biomarkers, GLP-1 receptor agonists, SCID, LBP, telemedicine, pulmonary embolism, image-guided robotic interventions, TBI, PASC, and cardiovascular risk calculators.
- Wrote a summary paper discussing definitions, clinical context, and significance of each term in modern healthcare practice.

## Weekly Discussion Insights
- Learned that AI in healthcare depends on carefully trained, tested, and validated systems before clinical use.
- Recognized that AI supports but does not replace clinical decision-making, highlighting the need for collaboration between technology and healthcare professionals.
- Emphasized the critical role of medical terminology (e.g., diagnosis, prognosis, pathology) in interpreting AI outputs accurately.
- Question raised: How do healthcare organizations continuously monitor AI systems after deployment to ensure ongoing accuracy as patient populations and medical practices evolve?

## Key Learnings
- Medical terminology is essential for clear communication, safe patient care, and effective AI integration.
- Understanding the clinical context of terms improves interpretation of research, collaboration across disciplines, and AI decision-support outcomes.
- Structured development and oversight of AI systems are crucial for patient safety and reliable results.
